Corona del Mar High School's Robotics Team

Based at Corona del Mar High School in Newport Beach, California, Triton Tech is a robotics team comprised of hardworking students, grades 9-12. In 2016, Triton Tech took part in FIRST, an international robotics competition. Despite being a first-year team, Triton Tech was named a finalist at the Orange County Regional, advancing to the World Championship in St. Louis, Missouri.
